About FoodBridge

FoodBridge is a platform dedicated to reducing food waste and fighting hunger by connecting food donors with organizations that distribute food to those in need.

Our mission is to create a sustainable ecosystem where surplus food is redirected to serve communities rather than ending up in landfills.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the food donation process work?

Organizations can donate surplus food through our platform, which is then collected by verified NGOs and distributed to those in need. The entire process is streamlined to ensure food reaches its destination quickly and safely.

Is there any cost involved?

Our platform is completely free for both donors and recipients. We're funded by grants and private donations to keep the service accessible to all who need it.

How do you ensure food safety?

All donations follow strict food safety guidelines and are monitored through our quality control system. We provide training resources for proper food handling and have temperature and time monitoring protocols in place.

Can individuals donate food?

Currently, we accept donations from restaurants, hotels, caterers, and food producers. We're working on expanding our platform to include individual donations in the future.

How can I volunteer for FoodBridge?

We're always looking for volunteers to help with food collection, delivery, and community outreach. Contact us through our website to learn about volunteer opportunities in your area.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the food donation process work?

Organizations can donate surplus food through our platform, which is then collected by verified NGOs and distributed to those in need.

Is there any cost involved?

Our platform is completely free for both donors and recipients.

How do you ensure food safety?

All donations follow strict food safety guidelines and are monitored through our quality control system.
